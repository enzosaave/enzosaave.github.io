Biography & Interests

My research focuses on understanding accretion processes in low-luminosity states. I am currently pursuing my Ph.D. under the supervision of Montserrat Armas Padilla and Teo Muñoz Darias at the Instituto de Astrofísica de Canarias (IAC).

The primary objective is to investigate accretion phenomena in low-mass X-ray binaries and ultracompact X-ray binaries (orbital periods < 80 min). I employ a multiwavelength approach (IR, Optical, X-ray) to decipher the physics of these extreme systems.
