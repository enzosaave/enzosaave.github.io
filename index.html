Biography

From Argentina to the Canary Islands

My research focuses on understanding accretion processes in low-luminosity states. I am currently pursuing my Ph.D. under the supervision of Montserrat Armas Padilla and Teo Muñoz Darias at the Instituto de Astrofísica de Canarias (IAC).

The primary objective of my thesis is to investigate accretion phenomena in low-mass X-ray binaries, including ultracompact X-ray binaries—a subtype characterized by orbital periods shorter than 80 minutes and composed of a neutron star and a degenerate companion. To achieve this, I employ a multiwavelength approach, combining infrared, optical, and X-ray observations to develop a comprehensive understanding of these systems.
