Biography

From Argentina to the Canary Islands

My research focuses on understanding accretion processes in low-luminosity states. I am currently pursuing my Ph.D. under the supervision of Montserrat Armas Padilla and Teo Muñoz Darias at the Instituto de Astrofísica de Canarias (IAC).

The primary objective of my thesis is to investigate accretion phenomena in low-mass X-ray binaries, specifically focusing on ultracompact X-ray binaries—systems with orbital periods shorter than 80 minutes. I employ a multiwavelength approach, combining infrared, optical, and X-ray observations to decode the physics of these extreme environments.
