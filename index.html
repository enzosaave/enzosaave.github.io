Biography

01 — ABOUT ME

My research focuses on understanding accretion processes in low-luminosity states. I am currently pursuing my Ph.D. under the supervision of Montserrat Armas Padilla and Teo Muñoz Darias at the Instituto de Astrofísica de Canarias (IAC).

The primary objective of my thesis is to investigate accretion phenomena in low-mass X-ray binaries, including ultracompact X-ray binaries—a subtype characterized by orbital periods shorter than 80 minutes. To achieve this, I employ a multiwavelength approach, combining infrared, optical, and X-ray observations.
